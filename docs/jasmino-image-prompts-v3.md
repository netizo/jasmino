## GLOBAL STYLE DIRECTIVES

Apply these to EVERY prompt as implicit context:

- **Color grading:** Cool-toned, slightly desaturated. Steel blues, warm tungsten highlights, deep shadows. Kodak Portra 400 pushed one stop.
- **Lighting:** Available-light industrial — overhead fluorescents, arc welding glow, natural light through factory skylights, workshop tungsten spots. NOT studio-lit.
- **Composition:** Documentary framing. Rule of thirds. Allow negative space. Include environment context (factory floor, scaffolding, pipe racks).
- **Mood:** Competence, scale, precision. These images prove "we do this work" — they're evidence, not decoration.
- **Texture:** Steel grain, rubber surfaces, weld beads, machining marks, bolt patterns. Surface detail matters.
- **Camera feel:** Shot on medium format or full-frame. Slight lens distortion acceptable for wide-angle factory shots. Imperfect, documentary, natural available lighting, not CGI, not 3D render, real photography.
- **People (when included):** Workers in proper PPE (hard hats, safety glasses, coveralls). Shown only as backs, silhouettes, gloved hands, or distant figures — faces never shown. Focused on work, not posing.
- **NO:** Lens flare, HDR look, oversaturation, stock-photo people, clean-room aesthetic (unless specified), AI art tells (too-perfect symmetry, melted fingers, impossible geometry).

### CRITICAL — EQUIPMENT SCALE REFERENCE

Jasmino's pressure vessels are ENORMOUS — comparable to a C-17 aircraft fuselage:

- **Diameter:** 5–8 meters (a person barely reaches the curvature)
- **Length:** 15–50 meters (5–15 story building laid on its side)
- **Wall thickness:** 25–60mm+ carbon or stainless steel plate
- **Weight:** 50–500+ tonnes per vessel
- **Crane capacity:** 100–500 tonnes required for handling
- **Fabrication halls:** Cathedral-scale (30m+ ceiling), shipyard dry dock proportions

**How to convey scale:** Always include people near equipment as small figures. Include standard-sized reference objects (vehicles, forklifts, stairways, scaffolding). Show multi-level scaffold platforms. Overhead cranes with visible tonnage ratings (100T, 200T).

- **Founded:** 1972 (Jasmino Elastomers Pvt Ltd, Maharashtra, India)
