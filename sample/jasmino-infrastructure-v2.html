Quality Infrastructure

One system.

Every facility operates under the same integrated quality management system. Same procedures, same documentation, same audit trail — regardless of geography.

01
Unified Documentation
Shared QMS, work instructions, and weld procedures across all sites. A document revised in India is live in Turkey the same day.
02
Cross-Site Audits
Regular internal audits between facilities. India audits HAW, HAW audits GBT, GBT audits India — circular accountability.
03
Digital Traceability
Material test reports, inspection records, and project documentation tracked from raw material to final delivery across all locations.
